What are MiOra’s Health Projects?

MiOra's health initiatives focus on awareness: of ovarian, endometrial cancer, multiple myeloma, and clinical trials. Empowering immigrants with better health access, bridging gaps in men's health understanding, and promoting longevity. Leveraging AI to reshape patient-physician dynamics, mental health awareness, preventive measures, and policy guidance.

Closing the Disparities in Cancer Program 

MiOra empowers communities! Our Health Ambassadors in callobration with GSK, trained 21 CSULA students in matters related to health issues and educated 300+ in LA on ovarian & endometrial cancers, multiple myeloma, and clinical trials.

We fight disparities: research published in ASCO and a webinar by Dr. Kristin Taylor target these issues.

MiOra’s Immigrant Health Literacy & Access Project 

Many immigrants struggle to navigate the healthcare system.This can make their lives harder and less healthy.

MiOra's student-run project bridges the gap, listening to immigrant needs and developing solutions for better health access. Everyone deserves good healthcare, regardless of background.

Assessing US Men’s Health Resource Utilization & the Associated Barriers Project 

Men's health awareness is on the rise, but knowledge gaps remain.

MiOra's nationwide study of 231 men dives deep to understand these gaps. They're investigating challenges in preventive care, accessing facilities, trusting doctors, and affording healthcare. This project paves the way for solutions that improve men's healthcare access

CMiOra’s AI project 

Concerned about navigating healthcare? MiOra explores how AI can revolutionize access and transform patient-doctor interactions. Imagine AI streamlining appointments, simplifying information, and empowering better care.

Stay tuned for insights on how AI is shaping a healthier future!

Mental Health Project

Mental health is a global issue, impacting students greatly.

We're researching if peer-to-peer programs (students helping students) can ease stress for new college/university students. These programs leverage the power of peer comfort to provide valuable support. Our past research on trust and decision-making informs this study, with the ultimate goal of promoting student well-being.

Healthy Long-Life Project – Preventative Care 

Knowledge empowers you to live your best life! Our program equips you with information on essential preventive measures - vaccines, screenings, healthy habits, and smart food choices. Make informed decisions and take control of your well-being.

Let's create a healthier future, one informed choice at a time.

Policy Project 

MiOra equips Health Ambassadors with the knowledge and skills to drive impactful policy change. We partner with industry leaders (like Pfizer's Norchelle M. Brown!) to offer comprehensive training.

Together, let's make a difference!
